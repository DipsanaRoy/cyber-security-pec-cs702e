## Unit 2: Hackers and Cyber Crimes

1. **Types of Hackers (Hat Colors):**
    * **White** Hat
    * **Black** Hat
    * **Grey** Hat
    * Script Kiddie
    * Hacktivist
    * State-Sponsored

2. **Hacker vs. Cracker:** Think of a **Cracker** as someone **cracking** a lock to steal.

3. **Virus vs. Worm:** A **Virus** needs a host like a biological virus. A **Worm** is a standalone parasite.

4. **Types of Insiders:** **M.N.C** or **I.N.C**
    * **M**alicious / **I**ntentional
    * **N**egligent
    * **C**ompromised

5. **Insider Analogy (Human Traits):**
    * **Lazy** 😴 = Negligent
    * **Good** 😇 = Compromised
    * **Evil** 😈 = Malicious

## Unit 3: Ethical Hacking & Social Engineering

1. **Phases of Ethical Hacking:** **Student Life Simulator**
    * **Recon** the syllabus
    * **Scan** what’s important
    * **Gain** marks
    * **Maintain access** by revision
    * **Cover tracks** by forgetting everything after exams
    * **Report** card

2. **Types of Phishing:** **SVWS** – Some Very Wicked Scams
    * **S**mishing
    * **V**ishing
    * **W**haling
    * **S**pear phishing

3. **Types of Social Engineering:** **PPT BQI** (Like a "PowerPoint BQI file")
    * **P**hishing
    * **P**retexting
    * **T**ailgating
    * **B**aiting
    * **Q**uid Pro Quo
    * **I**mpersonation

4. **Preventing Insider Threats (The 4D Shield):** **D.D.D.D**
    * **D**eny Excess Access
    * **D**etect Odd Behavior
    * **D**efend with Policies
    * **D**ouble-Check Identity

5. **Preventing Insider Threats (Alternative):** **ABP India**
    * **A**ccess
    * **B**ehavior
    * **P**olicies
    * **I**dentity

6. **Defense Against Social Engineering:** **AVSSI**
    * **A**wareness Training
    * **V**erification Culture
    * **S**imulated Attacks
    * **S**trong Policies
    * **I**ncident Reporting

7. **Defense Against Social Engineering (Alternative):** **VCAT PAIR 🐱**
    * **V**erification Culture
    * **C**ulture (Awareness)
    * **A**wareness (Training)
    * **T**raining (Policies)
    * **P**olicies (Attacks)

8. **OWASP Top 10 (Example):** **A01: Broken Access Control** – Remember by thinking of an unlocked door (**A**ccess).

## Unit 4: Cyber Forensics & Auditing

1. **Forensics Investigation Process:** *"Ident preserves CD collections while examine analyzes presentations"*
    * **Ident**ification
    * **Preserv**ation
    * **Collection**
    * **Examin**ation
    * **Analy**sis
    * **Present**ation

2. **Goals of Forensics:** **P.I.E.R.**
    * **P**reserve evidence
    * **I**dentify sources
    * **E**xtract artifacts
    * **R**eport clearly

3. **Core Principles of Forensics:** **C.A.R.E.**
    * **C**hain of custody
    * **A**uthenticity
    * **R**epeatability
    * **E**vidence integrity

4. **Types of Audits:** **ROTIE**
    * **R**ules (Compliance)
    * **O**perational
    * **T**echnical
    * **I**nternal
    * **E**xternal

5. **Audit Objectives:** **CIA, EC PC, MIA**
    * **CIA** (Confidentiality, Integrity, Availability)
    * **EC** (Effectiveness of Controls)
    * **PC** (Policy Compliance)
    * **MIA** (Misuse, Insider Threats, Anomalies)

6. **Audit Checklist:** **SCARF**
    * **S**tandards compliance
    * **C**ontrols check
    * **A**ccuracy of records
    * **R**isk identification
    * **F**ollow-ups

7. **Audit Planning Steps:** **SCOPE + CROPS**
    * **S**et audit **O**bjectives
    * **C**hoose **C**riteria
    * **O**rganize **S**cope
    * **P**repare **R**esources
    * **E**xecute **P**lan

8. **ISMS Core Elements (PDCA):** **Plan Do Check Act**

9. **ISMS Audit Process:** **PEP-CAR 🚗**
    * **P**lan
    * **E**valuate
    * **P**erform testing
    * **C**ompare
    * **A**nalyze
    * **R**eport

10. **ISO 27001 Key Clauses:** **CLIPS OP**

## Unit 5: Cyber Ethics and Laws

1. **IPSec provides:** **CIA** (Confidentiality, Integrity, Authentication)

2. **Intellectual Property Rights (IPR):** **C-P-T-T** – **"Cool Programmers Teach Tech"**
    * **C**ool → **C**opyright
    * **P**rogrammers → **P**atent
    * **T**each → **T**rademark
    * **T**ech → **T**rade Secret

3. **IT Act Penalties - Theme Memory Hooks:**
    * **3-Year Gang (Dishonesty):** Sections **65, 66, 66B, 66C, 66D, 72A** – **"66 = Dishonest Digital Deeds"**
    * **5-Year Club (Obscenity):** Sections **67, 67A, 67B** – **"67 = Naughty Stuff"**
    * **Life (Terrorism):** Section **66F** – **"F for Fear"**
    * **7-10 Years (vs. Govt):** Sections **69, 70** – **"Don't fight the GOV"**
    * **Compensation / Fines:** Sections **43, 43A, 44** – **"The 40s are about money"**
    * **2-Year Twins:** Sections **72, 73, 74** – **"The 70s twins for privacy and fraud"**

## General / Cross-Unit Mnemonics

1. **Encryption Algorithms:** **SSL DARE TLS**
    * **S**ecure Socket Layer
    * **D**ES
    * **A**ES
    * **R**SA
    * **E**CC
    * **T**ransport Layer Security

2. **Encryption vs. Hashing:**
    * **Encryption** = “Keep it secret.” 🔒
    * **Hashing** = “Keep it safe from tampering.” 🛡️

3. **IDS vs. IPS:**
    * **IDS** = CCTV camera (Detects & Alerts)
    * **IPS** = Security guard (Detects & Blocks)

4. **Certifying Authority Analogy:** **CCA (Dad)** -> **CAs (Kids)** -> **RCAI (Grandparent)**
